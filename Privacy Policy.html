Privacy Policy – AI Buddy

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Overview

AI Buddy respects your privacy. This app is designed to help you with goals, motivation, and daily check-ins without collecting personal information.

Data We Store

  • Mood selections
  • Tone preferences
  • Goals and progress
  • Streaks and badges
  • App settings (e.g., theme, Smart Mode)

All of this data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any server.

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not collect personal data
  • We do not sell or share data
  • We do not track you across apps or websites

Third-Party Services

AI Buddy may optionally integrate third-party services in the future (for example, AI APIs). If that happens, this policy will be updated before any data is shared.

Your Control

You can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.
